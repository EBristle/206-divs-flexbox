Hi. I'm Emily.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who creates thoughtful and user-oriented designs. When I'm not working, you can find me exploring the streets and vineyards of Mendoza, Argentina, where wine, steak and ice cream are sacred.

From a young age, I knew that I wanted to see the world. For some reason, flamenco dancing was the thing that first sparked my interest in other cultures. I saved up to travel to Spain when I was sixteen, and from then on, I was hooked on exploring new places.

I spent a semester in Argentina in college, and then joined the Peace Corps with my husband after we graduated. Upon moving back to the U.S., we based ourselves in New York City, but ended up working on humanitarian assignments overseas for months at a time.

Overall, we spent over five years in Africa, and many months in South America, amongst other places. We love exploring new locations and cultures, but we are always looking for the perfect place to call home. Learning coding lets me continue on our quest while pursuing a new career.
